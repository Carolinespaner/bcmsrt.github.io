About MSRT

Mission

The Western Canada COVID-19 Medical Student Response Team (WE-CAN COVID-19 MSRT) is a student-led, grassroots initiative with the primary objective of empowering medical students to support physicians and public health authorities in their efforts against COVID-19.

Contact

You can contact us at wecanmsrt@gmail.com with any questions or inquiries.
